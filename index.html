Pet Glam Application

Welcome to the Pet Glam Application. Upload an image and generate a prompt to create new images of your pet. Also, be sure to check out the 3D model generator to see your pet in a whole new dimension and use it to print a actual 3D keychain model.




About

The Pet Glam Application is a web application that allows users to upload an image of their pet and generate a prompt to create new images of their pet. We have a variety of filters to choose from, so play around and see what you like. The application uses a pre-trained StyleGAN2 model to generate new images of the pet based on the prompt. Users can choose from a variety of filters to generate images. The application also includes a 3D model generator that allows users to see their pet in a 3D model and print a 3D keychain model of their pet.

3D Model Object Creation

This filter will take a picture of your pup or kitten and turn them into a 3D .object file from which you can upload to your 3D printer program and print a key chain size model of your pet.
